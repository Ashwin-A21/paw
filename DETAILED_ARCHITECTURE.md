## Detailed Data Flow Descriptions
- **Authentication**: Data flow for user login, registration, and password recovery processes.
- **Pet Management**: Details the processes related to adding, updating, and deleting pet profiles.
- **Adoption**: Processes outlining the adoption request, approval, and tracking.
- **Rescue**: Information flow regarding pet rescue operations and volunteer management.
- **Blog**: Data flow concerning blog creation, updates, and user interaction.
- **Donation**: Details the processes for donation tracking and management.
- **Admin**: Administrative tasks concerning user roles and system configurations.
- **Volunteer**: Processes for volunteer onboarding, tracking, and feedback.
